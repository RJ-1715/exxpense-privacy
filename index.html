Exxpense — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 15 June 2026 · Last updated: 15 June 2026

Exxpense ("the app", "we", "us") is an automatic expense tracker for Android, operated by Akshay Jaiswal (the developer). This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, how it is protected, and your choices. It applies to the app published on Google Play under the package name com.exxpense. You can contact the developer at any time at exxpenseapp@gmail.com.

SMS data and how we use it

Exxpense reads incoming bank and UPI transaction SMS on your device to automatically detect financial transactions. SMS messages are processed entirely on your device to extract transaction details such as the amount, the merchant, the last four digits of the account, and the date. The full text of your SMS is never transmitted off your device, never shared with third parties, and never sold.

The app reads only bank and UPI transaction alerts. One-time passwords (OTPs), personal messages, and any SMS that is not a bank transaction are ignored and are not turned into transactions.

What data the app accesses

The app accesses the following categories of data, and processes all of them on your device only:

  • SMS messages — incoming bank and UPI transaction alerts, read on your device to detect transactions. The message text is not uploaded.
  • Financial information — the transaction details derived from those SMS (amount, merchant, the last four digits of the account, and date). This information is created and stored on your device.

Exxpense does not access your contacts, location, photos, files, call logs, camera, microphone, or device identifiers, and it does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

What we collect and store

In this version, all data — the SMS the app reads and the transactions it creates — is stored locally on your device only. We do not operate a server that receives your SMS or transaction data, and we do not upload, sync, or back up this data anywhere. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Permissions we request

  • Receive SMS (RECEIVE_SMS) — used solely to detect incoming bank transaction messages and create transactions from them. This is the core function of the app; without it the app cannot work. We ask for SMS access with a clear on-screen explanation before the Android system prompt appears.
  • Internet (INTERNET) — a standard Android permission required for the app to run. In this version it is not used to send your SMS or transaction data anywhere; your personal data stays on your device.

Data sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your SMS messages or transaction data to any third party. No personal data leaves your device.

Data security

Because your SMS and transaction data never leave your device, it is not transmitted over the internet and is not stored on any server we control. The data remains within the app's private storage on your device and is protected by your device's own security, such as your screen lock and Android's built-in storage encryption.

Data retention and deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you are in full control of it. You can delete all of the app's data at any time by clearing the app's storage in Android Settings (Settings → Apps → Exxpense → Storage → Clear data) without uninstalling, or by uninstalling the app. Since we do not hold any copy of your data on a server, there is no server-side data to request deletion of. If you have any questions about deleting your data, you can contact us at the email address below.

Children

Exxpense is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices change in a future version — for example, if optional cloud sync is added — this policy will be updated before that version is released, the "Last updated" date above will change, and the change will be reflected in the app's Play Store Data Safety section.
